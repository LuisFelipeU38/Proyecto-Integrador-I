User Stories:
1.	As a user, I want to be able to register on the website to access its features.
2.	As a user, I want to be able to log in to the website with my username and password to access exclusive features.
3.	As a user, I want to receive a confirmation email after logging in or registering on the website to verify my successful login.
4.	As a user, I want to be able to view an informative dashboard on the website that shows me relevant news, events, and updates related to football academies.
5.	As a user, I want to have the ability to record and update my personal information in my profile, such as my address, contact number, and relevant details.
6.	As a player user, I want to be able to view my personal data, including my name, age, position, etc., in my profile.
7.	As a coach user, I want to be able to enroll player users in sports events, providing relevant information about the event and selecting participating players.
8.	As a coach user, I want to have the ability to edit the statistics of player users, such as goals scored, assists, yellow cards, red cards, etc., to maintain an accurate record of their performance.
9.	As a coach user, I want to be able to create sports events, providing details such as date, time, location, and event rules, so that player users can register.
10.	As an administrator user, I want to have the ability to update the payment status of player users, marking whether they have made their respective payments or if they have pending payments.
